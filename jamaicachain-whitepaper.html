JamaicanChain: A Decentralized Digital Infrastructure for Jamaica's Economic Future

Abstract

This white paper introduces JamaicanChain, a modified Bitcoin-based blockchain infrastructure designed to accelerate Jamaica's digital transformation and establish the nation as a Caribbean blockchain hub. It includes:

  • Parish-based validation
  • Cultural heritage preservation
  • Seamless integration with Jamaica's CBDC (JAM-DEX)

1. Introduction

1.1 Vision

JamaicanChain aims to create a robust digital infrastructure that powers Jamaica's transition into a leading blockchain economy while preserving its unique cultural identity and fostering financial inclusion.

1.2 Background

As blockchain technology reshapes the global financial landscape, Jamaica stands at a crucial junction. Blockchain technology offers unprecedented opportunities for economic growth and financial inclusion.

2. Technical Architecture

2.1 Core Implementation

JamaicanChain builds upon Bitcoin Core's proven architecture while introducing several key modifications:

2.1.1 Block Structure

class CJamaicanBlockHeader { // Bitcoin Core original fields int32_t nVersion; uint256 hashPrevBlock; uint256 hashMerkleRoot; uint32_t nTime; uint32_t nBits; uint32_t nNonce; // Jamaican-specific fields uint8_t nParishID; uint256 hashIdentityRef; uint256 hashCulturalData; };

2.2 Consensus Mechanism

JamaicanChain implements a Delegated Proof of Stake (DPoS) consensus mechanism with parish-based validation, combining the efficiency of DPoS with geographical decentralization.

2.2.1 DPoS Implementation

  • Block Producers: 42 active block producers (3 from each of the 14 parishes)
  • Block Time: 3 seconds
  • Round Duration: 126 seconds (42 producers Ã— 3 seconds)
  • Stake Requirement: Minimum 100,000 JMC to qualify as a block producer
  • Voting Power: Proportional to staked amount and staking duration

4. Economic Model

4.1 Token Economics

  • Native token: JMC (Jamaica Coin)
  • Total supply: 1 billion JMC
  • Initial distribution:
    • 40% - Public sale
    • 20% - Parish development fund
    • 15% - Ecosystem development
    • 15% - Team and advisors (3-year vesting)
    • 10% - Reserve fund
  • Staking rewards: 5-12% APR based on stake amount and duration
  • Parish bonus: Additional 2% for maintaining geographical diversity
